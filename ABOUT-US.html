ABOUT KARMVEER VITTAL RAMJI SHINDE SANSTHA'S

The year 1974 shivraj college was handed over to Karmveer Vitthal Ramaji Shinde Shikshan Sanstha, Gadhinglaj. Hon. Shri. Balasaheb alias R. S. Mane, the president of Karmveer Vitthal Ramaji Shinde Shikshan Sanstha, Gadhinglaj gave the dynamic leadership to this institute. He tried his best to fulfill the educational needs of the students of this area by introducing the faculty of science both at junior and senior college level. shri. d. s. kadam then vice-president donated rs. two lac to meet the expenses of the faculty of science. hon. shri. balasaheb alias r. s. mane, shri. d. s. kadam concentrated on the construction of new buildings. the foundation stone of the new building was laid at the hands of hon. yashavantrao chavan and hon. vasantdada patil the then chief minister of maharashtra.
